Our Story

R.K. Scientific Industries was founded in 1965 with "Chhapak" brand in 2022 with a vision to revolutionize the personalization industry through cutting-edge laser printing technology. What started as a small workshop in Ambala has now grown into a leading provider of custom laser printing services across India.

Our brand "Chhapak" was launched in 2015 to cater specifically to the growing demand for personalized gifts and products. The name "Chhapak" (meaning "impression" in Hindi) represents our commitment to making a lasting impression through our quality products and services.

Over the years, we have invested in state-of-the-art laser technology and skilled craftsmen to deliver products that exceed customer expectations. Our dedication to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry.
